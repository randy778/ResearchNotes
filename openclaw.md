# OpenClaw

## What is it?
OpenClaw is an open-source personal AI assistant that runs locally on your machine, executing real-world tasks like reading files, running commands, and automating workflows. It acts as an autonomous agent integrated with LLMs (Claude, GPT, DeepSeek) that you can interact with through messaging platforms like Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, and Telegram.

## Key Ideas
- **Local Execution**: Runs directly on your machine, keeping data under your control rather than in cloud infrastructure.
- **Task Automation**: Executes actual tasks beyond conversation—reads/modifies files, runs scripts, controls browsers, manages workflows.
- **Multi-Channel Integration**: Connects with messaging apps (Slack, Discord, WhatsApp, Telegram) for remote interaction without direct machine access.

## Popular Tools
- **LLM Models**: Works with Claude, GPT-4, DeepSeek for reasoning and decision-making.
- **AgentSkills**: Over 100 preconfigured skills for shell commands, file management, and web automation.
- **Third-Party Integrations**: Supports 50+ integrations including smart home hardware, productivity suites, and music platforms.

## Why Use It?
OpenClaw democratizes AI automation for personal and team workflows by enabling task execution beyond simple chat—you can automate repetitive work, integrate with your existing tools, and maintain privacy through local execution. It's model-agnostic, free to self-host, and allows you to build custom automation without cloud vendor lock-in.
